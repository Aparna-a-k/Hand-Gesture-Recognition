1)Title: Hand Gesture recognition Database.

2)Source: Version 1,created by Tomas Mantecon

3)Information about data:
     Hand gesture recognition database is presented, composed by a set of 
     near infrared images acquired by the Leap Motion sensor. 
     The dataset is composed of 10 different hand gestures by 10 different subjects(Men and women)
     There are 10 different folders which contains sub-folders each containing different hand gestures like:
     'palm','fist','fist moved','thumb','index','ok','palm moved','c','I','down'.
     Each folder conatins 200 images each.


4)Dataset link:
https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/gti-upm/leapgestrecog
